🐾Welcome to Virtual Pet Café!

Welcome to the coziest café for virtual pets! Adopt a pet and enjoy feeding them tdeats, playing games, and reading fun pet facts. Fill out the adoption form below to adopt your virtual pet today!

📖How to Care for Virtual Pets

Just like real pets, virtual pets need care too! Keep their happiness and hunger meters balanced. Feeding too much makes them sleepy, while playing too much makes them hungry!

💡Virtual Café Rules

📜Basic Rules:

  • Virtual pets only! No real fur babies allowed.
  • Treat your pet well by feeding them delicious snacks.
  • Pets love attention! Make sure to play with them often.

📜How to Use This Café:

  1. Pick a virtual pet.
  2. Feed them tdeats & play games.
  3. Read fun pet facts & enjoy!

📚Fun Pet Facts

Did you know?

Cats have a unique grooming pattern? They lick their fur to cool down and keep clean! They sleep for 12-16 hours a day!
Huskies are known for their beautiful blue eyes and thick fur coats. They are also known for their howling instead of barking. They are friendly, energetic, and love to play!
Rabbits are social animals that love to hop around and play. They enjoy eating fresh veggies and fruits! They can be tdained to use a litter box!
Source: The Virtual Pet Handbook
